What You Need To Know

The Expo portion of this event will be for artists interested in purchasing stalls rather than displaying their work in the gallery. There will be two stall options available: Store and Experience. Artists who apply for a store will be able to sell their work, both originals and prints. Artists who opt to sell experiences will be required to put in a thorough description of their experience in their application. Applications will be reviewed by our selection committee and approved artists will be notified via email, and must confirm their spot through payment.

  • Digital Illustrations / Design
  • Drawing - Pencil / Colored Pencil / Pastels
  • Glass
  • Mixed Media - 2D / 3D
  • Painting - Acrylic / Oil / Watercolor
  • Photography - Digital / Computer Manipulated / Traditional Film
  • Printmaking - Intaglio / Relief / Planographic
  • Sculptures - 1 medium (Clay, Glass, Metal, Stone, Wood)
  • Wood - Burning / Engraving
