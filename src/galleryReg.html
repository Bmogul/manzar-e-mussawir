The Gallery

The Gallery will be a place for artists to display their best works to new audiences, giving them a chance to grow as creators. Participating artists will be given the choice to put their pieces up for a silent auction, in which they will get 90% of the commission and the rest will go towards upliftment. Gallery artists will be able to sell their work ONLY if they have put up their work for auction, otherwise, the art will simply be displayed and can be sold after the event is over. Interested artists can apply using the link above and must submit 3 pieces which will then be reviewed by a jury. It is important to us to ensure no bias is included in the judging of these pieces, hence we request artists to crop out, blur, or remove their signatures before submitting their pieces. Our jury members will only be given images of artwork, no names. Selected artists will be notified via email, and must confirm their spot through payment. Artists will not be required to be present on the event day. **Note: Artwork displayed is not required to be those that were submitted*** Â
