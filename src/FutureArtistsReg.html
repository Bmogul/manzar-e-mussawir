What You Need To Know

We welcome our young future artists with an opportunity to participate in this event! Imaam Moiz AS displayed the connection between art and faith when depicting a design of the Tree of Life in the Mehraab of AlJamea al Azhar. Following in his footsteps, the 52nd Dai al Mutlaq, Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin RA portrayed the same design in the Mehraab of AlJamea al Anwar.

We invite our young artists to use their talents to depict AlShajarat ul Hayaat, or the Tree of Life. We want to make sure we can display as many pieces as possible, so we request that your child's artwork is no bigger than 8x10 inches. Future Artists applications will be accepted on a first come first serve basis, no artwork submission will be required. Further instructions will be provided post registration!

  • Digital Illustrations
  • Drawing - Pencil / Colored Pencil / Pastels
  • Mixed Media - 2D
  • Painting - Acrylic / Oil / Watercolor
  • Printmaking - Intaglio / Relief / Planographic
