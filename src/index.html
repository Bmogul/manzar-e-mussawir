Manzar-e-Mussawir

Welcome

Talebaat ul Mumenaat Houston would like to present the first-ever art gallery and expo to shine a light on our amazing, talented North American Mumineen artists! With this event, we hope to be able to bring more attention and appreciation to the arts in our community.  In a reference to the value of art and faith, His Holiness Syedna AaliQadr Mufaddal Saifuddin TUS states, "In the field of art and literature, he who remembers Allah Taa'la, strives for Aakherat, and mentions Allah Taa'la in his work, will forever experience balance and value in his art."  

The Gallery

Step into our captivating art gallery, where the walls breathe with the ingenuity of our talented artists. Here, creativity knows no bounds, with vibrant paintings, breathtaking photographs, intricate calligraphy, and more, displaying the love our artists have for Allah Taa'la and His creations, from depictions of nature to the world of Islamic art. Participating artists will be given a choice between simply displaying their work or opting in for a silent auction where a percentage of their commissions will go towards uplifting those less fortunate than us.

The Expo

Within these immersive walls, artists will have the opportunity to offer visitors the chance to not only acquire beautiful artworks, may they be originals or prints, but also purchase experiences such as photoshoots or live painting lessons. This expo will allow visitors to spark boundless creativity as they explore and connect with our artists, making it a memorable experience for people of all ages, young and old.

Future Artists

A heartwarming corner of our gallery will be dedicated to the imaginative masterpieces of the younger generation. This section will showcase the creations of budding artists embracing the theme of "Al Shajarat ul Hayaat," or the Tree of Life. We hope this opportunity encourages our young artists to explore their creativity and brighten the world with their visions.
